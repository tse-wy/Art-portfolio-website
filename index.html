About

Nicole Nichols

A former Silhouette and Caricature Artist at Tokyo Disney Sea. And a heart full of love for cats.
Nicole is a digital Artist working on personal projects that range from self-directed illustrations to commissions. Her talent has not been unnoticed and she has been featured on ImagineFX.
Her intricate and detailed silhouette works are carefully hand cut using surgical scissors to achieve her designs and achieve her creative vision through meticulous means. She can cut a single person silhouette in 5 minutes and draw a caricature in 5 minutes or less.
